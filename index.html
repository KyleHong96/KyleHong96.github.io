Our platform is the final result of 20 years of experience in the fields of web development and multimedia. Based on the popular Raspberry PI single board pc, our software take the best from all the hardware features (WIFI intergrated, fast SSD writer/reader, Broadcom GPU to decode H264 video) giving the best performance and the best FULL HD Experience.

Our player never stops, keeping on playing even without internet connection thanks to it's internal SSD card, up to 128GB.

The Server Admin panel is hosted on the cloud, for HA and business continuity.

It allows you to:
Upload and manage your assets (any video format, any picture format, html5 zipped layout, MP3 and M4a audio files)

Create your playlists with different and fully customizable layouts

Deploy to your player, following a schedule(up to 30 schedule playlists in the same moment) or to play immediately.

Monitor your player: The communication with the players is in realtime and bidirectional. The player will regularly communicate it's status and what it' s playing. From the server you can check in any moment any details of the player and also have a realtime snapshot of te screen.

All inclusive formula: everything with a small montly or annual fee. more Player you get, more you save. 2 years of minimum contract time.
